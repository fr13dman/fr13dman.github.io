## Why this blog exists

After years in software engineering and big tech, I'm using this space to think out loud about what comes next. Writing helps me clarify my own thinking, connect dots across ideas, and leave behind notes that may be useful to someone else working through similar questions.

If a post here feels unfinished, exploratory, or a little opinionated, that is probably by design. This is a working notebook, not a press release.
